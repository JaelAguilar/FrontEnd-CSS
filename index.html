Why should I vaccine?

In order to avoid any doubts about getting the COVID-19 vaccine, identify the following 4 benefits of COVID-19 vaccination:

The success of vaccines is strongly influenced by the strength of the body's immunde system. Therefore, there are several things that can be tried to make the COVID-19 vaccine work:

Avoid alcoholic beverages
Avoid stress
Eat healthy food
Get enough sleep
Exercise or physical activity
